About the Symposium

The International Symposium on Forage Quality and Conservation (ISFQC) was established to promote scientific discussion, knowledge exchange, and international collaboration in the fields of forage quality, conservation strategies, and sustainable livestock production systems.

Organized by a multidisciplinary research group with strong academic and institutional connections, the symposium brings together researchers, industry professionals, extension specialists, and students to discuss advances in forage science, preservation techniques, and nutritional evaluation.

ISFQC has consolidated itself as a scientific forum committed to excellence, innovation, and the dissemination of high-quality research, strengthening the dialogue between academia, industry, and society.

Scientific Program

The scientific program will feature keynote lectures, oral and poster presentations, and round-table discussions focused on forage production, conservation technologies, quality assessment, and sustainable feeding systems.
