International Symposium on Forage Quality and Conservation

About the Symposium

It is with great pleasure that we present the VII International Symposium on Forage Quality and Conservation (VII ISFQC), an event that has become one of the main scientific and technical references in forage conservation, animal nutrition, and innovation in production systems. In its seventh edition, the symposium will bring together researchers, industry professionals, students, and leading companies that operate in livestock, agriculture, and the broader agribusiness sector.

The ISFQC aims to promote high-level knowledge exchange, strengthen partnerships, and stimulate technological advances that support the sustainable development of animal production. This edition will address updated discussions on forage quality, conservation strategies, monitoring technologies, laboratory analysis, pasture management, and market trends.

ISFQC has consolidated itself as a scientific symposium committed to excellence, innovation, and the dissemination of high-quality research, strengthening the dialogue between academia, industry, and society.

Scientific Program

The scientific program will feature keynote lectures, oral and poster presentations, and round-table discussions focused on forage production, conservation technologies, quality assessment, and sustainable feeding systems.

Abstract Submission

The ISFQC 2026 invites the submission of scientific abstracts addressing advances in forage quality, conservation technologies, evaluation methods, and sustainable livestock systems.

Abstracts must be submitted in English and will be evaluated by the Scientific Committee based on originality, scientific merit, and relevance to the symposium themes.

Detailed submission guidelines, templates, and deadlines will be announced soon.
